Fluffy Pancakes

Home About Pancakes Garlic Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in egg, milk, and butter.
  3. Heat pan and pour batter to form pancakes.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, then flip. Serve warm!
